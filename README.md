# ⚛️ Quantum Challenge 2025

> The **Quantum Challenge 2025** is a competition hosted by the **Quantum Information Research Support Center**. Participants solve three problems designed directly by experts from IonQ, a cloud-based quantum computing company specializing in ion-trap technology. Through this process, participants expand their knowledge of quantum information and quantum computing while building professional networks. Certificates are awarded at different levels based on the number of correctly solved problems.
